About

What do you think when you hear the word 'tandem'?
You might think of the tandem bicycle, popular in the 19th century, which allowed two people to ride a bicycle together.
You might also think of the more modern definition when two learners of foreign languages come together to practice and help each other
Here at tanDev, we took the core concept of tandem as a combined effort and skill exchange to help connect developers and designers who want to both teach and learn from others.
Know Python, but bad with design?
Or maybe an old-school dev with masterful PHP skills but no idea of all these newfangled languages?
Create a profile and search for other people with the skills you desire to get started exchanging your skills now! Once connected, it's up to you to figure out a tandem that works best for you, be that over text, voice or in person.*
* tanDev's zipcode search currently only works for Berlin.
